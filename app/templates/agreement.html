COUNCIL COMPLIANCE

Expanders cannot provide assistance with the process of obtaining permits or approvals for the long-term installation, parking, or connection of caravan services on a specific property. The reason for this is the variation in legislation from council to council, which makes it challenging to determine the necessary application or approval process. Expanders also cannot guarantee compliance with the regulations of any local council area.

If you put an Expander on a trailer they are considered as a Caravan (movable dwelling) and can be conveniently installed in numerous councils without requiring approval from the council.
